# Credit Default Dataset

## Summary
This dataset contains information about credit card clients and whether they defaulted on their next month’s payment. It includes demographic, credit, and payment history features. It is suitable for risk analysis and predictive modeling.

## Variables
- Numerical:LIMIT_BAL, AGE, BILL_AMT1, BILL_AMT2, BILL_AMT3, BILL_AMT4, BILL_AMT5, BILL_AMT6, PAY_AMT1, PAY_AMT2, PAY_AMT3, PAY_AMT4, PAY_AMT5, PAY_AMT6
- Categorical:SEX, EDUCATION, MARRIAGE, PAY_0, PAY_2, PAY_3, PAY_4, PAY_5, PAY_6
- Target variable:default payment next month (0 = no default, 1 = default)

## Rows and Columns
- Rows:30,001
- Columns:25

## Why it’s good for enterprise decision-making
- Helps identify customers likely to default on loans
- Useful for credit risk management and financial decision-making
- Can be used to perform descriptive analytics, visualize trends, and build predictive models

## Suggested Business Question
- Which customers are most likely to default on their loans next month?
